Chicago Tech Workers Coalition

Who We Are:

We are a coalition of workers in and around the tech industry, labor organizers, community organizers, and friends located within the Chicagoland area.

What We Do:

We held tech circles which were groups for workers to explore issues in the workplace, community, and industry and how tech workers can organize for power. Sadly due to COVID-19 and coordinator exhaustion, we were unable to organize in 2020, but we are hoping to start again soon.
